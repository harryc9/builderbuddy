## Technical SEO Checklist

### On-Page SEO (Per Article)

**Title Tags:**
- Format: "Primary Keyword | 416permits"
- Length: 50-60 characters
- Include location (Toronto, GTA)
- Example: "Toronto Building Permits: 2025 Guide | 416permits"

**Meta Descriptions:**
- Length: 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Clear call-to-action
- Example: "Search 300K+ Toronto building permits by trade, cost & location. Free 7-day trial. Find HVAC, electrical, plumbing projects daily."

**URL Structure:**
- Clean, keyword-rich slugs
- Format: `/blog/primary-keyword`
- Example: `/blog/toronto-building-permits-guide`

**Headers (H1, H2, H3):**
- H1: Primary keyword (one per page)
- H2: Related keywords, question variations
- H3: Supporting details
- Example:
  - H1: Toronto Building Permits: Complete 2025 Guide
  - H2: How to Search Toronto Building Permits
  - H3: Using the City of Toronto Portal

**Internal Linking:**
- Link to 3-5 related blog posts
- Link to signup page (soft CTA in content)
- Link to homepage
- Use keyword-rich anchor text

**External Linking:**
- Link to authoritative sources (City of Toronto, government sites)
- 2-3 external links per article
- Opens in new tab
- NoFollow for low-quality sources

**Images:**
- Alt text with keywords: "toronto building permit search dashboard"
- Compressed (WebP format)
- Descriptive filenames: `toronto-permits-dashboard.webp`
- Screenshot examples from your app

**Content Length:**
- Pillar posts: 3000-4000 words
- Standard posts: 1500-2500 words
- Tutorials: 2000-3000 words
- Updates: 800-1200 words
