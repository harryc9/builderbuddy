## A/B TEST IDEAS

Test different:
1. **Subject lines:** Question vs Statement vs Curiosity
2. **Email length:** Short (100 words) vs Long (300 words)
3. **CTA:** "Free trial" vs "Test it free" vs "Worth a test?"
4. **Personalization:** High vs Low
5. **Tone:** Professional vs Casual vs Founder-y

Track which gets highest:
- Open rate (subject line test)
- Reply rate (body test)
- Click rate (CTA test)

---

## FINAL TIPS

**DO:**
- ✅ Write like a human (use contractions, be casual)
- ✅ Use their first name naturally (not just "Hi {{firstName}}")
- ✅ Include specific examples (actual addresses, costs)
- ✅ Make it scannable (short paragraphs, bullets)
- ✅ End with a question (increases replies)

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Use marketing jargon ("revolutionize", "disrupt", "cutting-edge")
- ❌ Make it about you ("We're the leading...", "We've helped...")
- ❌ Be vague ("Save time", "Increase efficiency")
- ❌ Use multiple CTAs (one clear action only)
- ❌ Sound like a template (even though it is)
